Find stores in your neighborhood whose food has reached the "sell by" date. FoodMap shows you which food items they are getting rid of and navigates you to their location.

Turns out FoodMap helps prevents waste and is a great way for those in a low tax bracket to get good food for a free or discounted price.
It also addresses the need for grocery stores to keep a better online database of when they stock food and the sell-by dates on their food.
Created using the EveryBlock API and the Google Maps API.
